Sources
Hundreds of hours have gone into individually researching the social, environmnental and potential health impacts of almost 100 foods. In our research, we made sure to only use peer reviewed, published works from reputable sources and organizations including Pub Med, Science Direct, the USDA, the Water Footprint Network, and Environmental Working Group. Note that the metrics used are average figures and the actual resources required to produce food sources can vary depending on different characteristics including distance from farm to table, amount of pesticides/herbicides used, certified organic status, as well as grass-fed/humanely raised labels to name a few. If you have any additional questions, feel free to contact us.

    The food ingredient calculator is designed to objectively illustrate how our diets have such a significant impact. Just by allowing people to be conscious of the actual costs and resources required to create their meal, we hope to help reduce the negative impacts our diets have.

    The daily meal calculator is designed to help users understand the impacts of their average diet on a larger scale, comparing what they usually eat in a day to the average US/American diet. By doing this, we help users understand their footprint compared to others and also help visualize the potentially massive impacts that we could have on the world if everyone in the US ate a similar diet to the user.
